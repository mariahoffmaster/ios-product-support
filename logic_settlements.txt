OVERVIEW
--------
Remittance reports are monthly reports Unlock provides to investors that track the transfer
of funds from one party to another as required by each Asset Management Agreement (AMA).
They detail the collections from settlements each month for each trust, and the waterfall
payments made from those collections per the trust's AMA.

Unlock also provides monthly position tapes, borrowing base tapes (for LibreMax/EWB),
and equity balance certifications (for LibreMax State Street reporting).

KEY TERMINOLOGY
---------------
- AMA — Asset Management Agreement. Governs fees and waterfall payments per trust.
- Remittance — Monthly transfer of settlement collections through the investor waterfall.
- AM Fee — Asset Management Fee paid by investor to Unlock.
- Disposition Fee — Fee paid on settlement/disposition of an asset.
- Incentive Fee — Performance-based fee per AMA terms.
- Premium Recapture — Recovery of premium paid on assets at origination.
- Settlement Fee — $500 fee per settlement (new; added to remittance in updated process).
- Lien Release Fee — $75 per settlement.
- Reimbursable Expenses — Costs Unlock incurred managing the assets, reimbursed by investor.
- Exercise Value — Investor's portion of settlement proceeds per HEA terms.
- Excess Servicing / MSR — Sold mortgage servicing rights. Excess servicing fees flow to
  MSR Purchaser Account (SGOF) after investor remittance.
- Trustee — Third-party fiduciary appointed to represent investor's interests.
- Administrator — Manages administrative tasks on behalf of investor.
- Directional Letter — Document instructing the Trustee to move funds (used for auction bids).
- EWB — East West Bank. Provides borrowing base facility for LibreMax.
- Borrowing Base — LibreMax/EWB calculation requiring monthly position tape and defaults tape.
- State Street — Custodian/administrator for LibreMax equity balance reporting.
- SGOF — Saluda Grade MSR purchaser account. Receives excess servicing post-remittance.


REMITTANCE FLOW OF FUNDS
--------------------------
(From Remittance Flow of Funds diagram — New Process)

1. Unlock sends each investor a monthly collections report on the 15th of each month.
   Report includes breakdown of:
   - AM Fees
   - Disposition Fees
   - Incentive Fees
   - Premium Recapture
   - Settlement Fees (new)
   - Reimbursable Expenses

2. Investors remit payment to Unlock by the 25th of the month.
   Funds flow: Investor Trust Holdings Account → UAM

3. Once all investor remits are received, Unlock prepares MSR collection report.
   Excess servicing fees transferred to MSR Purchaser Account (SGOF).

4. Retained fees + Settlement Fees transferred to UAM TCB account.

5. All funds flow to: UAM MSR Clearing Account (TCB) for distribution.


LIBREMAX BORROWING BASE
------------------------
LibreMax provides borrowing base information to East West Bank (EWB) monthly.
Unlock provides two tapes each month: (1) position tape, (2) defaults tape.

Accounts: LEX 1 (account 7) and LEX 2 (account 8).

Position Tape Steps:
1. Run script: A:\00 - Operations\Scripts\New Tape Format v8-LibreMax Borrowing Base.sql
   Set cutoff date and confirm account filter:
     SET @cutoff = 'MM/DD/YYYY'
     insert into @accounts (item) values (7) -- LEX 1
     insert into @accounts (item) values (8) -- LEX 2

2. Copy results to Excel. Remove any duplicate asset IDs (from multiple default records).

3. Copy format from previous month's tape.
   Example: A:\04 - Investors\LEX1\Monthly Reporting\202411\
            LEX 1 - Borrowing Base Position Tape - 11.30.2024.xlsx

4. Split tape for LEX 1 and LEX 2 assets; copy to respective month-end folders.

Defaults Tape Steps:
1. Run script: A:\00 - Operations\Scripts\LibreMax-BorrowingBase-Defaults.sql
   Produces list of defaults for both LEX 1 and LEX 2 accounts.

2. Copy to Excel. Update "Chapter/Type" values using this mapping:
   Tape Name                    → Update to
   Senior Mortgage Foreclosure  → FCL - Senior Lender
   Chapter 7 Bankruptcy         → BK - 7
   Chapter 13 Bankruptcy        → BK - 13
   Property Tax Foreclosure     → FCL - Property Tax
   Federal Tax Liens            → Federal Tax Liens

3. Copy format from previous tape. Both LEX 1 and LEX 2 can stay on the same tape.

4. Update "Closure Type" for any defaults with a cure date:
   - Bankruptcies: Standard Discharge
   - FCLs: Confirm with Special Servicing team ("Notice of Rescission" or "Standard Discharge")

Tape file save location: A:\04 - Investors\LEX1\Monthly Reporting\[YYYYMM]\

AUCTION BID FUNDS PROCESS
---------------------------
When an Unlock asset is scheduled for foreclosure, bid funds must be secured for auction.

Glossary:
  - Directional Letter: Document instructing Trustee to move funds.
  - Trustee: Third-party fiduciary for the investor.
  - Administrator: Manages administrative tasks on behalf of investor.
  - AMA: Asset Management Agreement.

Step 1: Prepare Directional Letter (Portfolio Management)
  Navigate to 04 - Investors folder → Account folder → Directional Letter template.
  Update: dollar amount, date funds needed by, asset ID.
  Send for signature via DocuSign to: Unlock CFO and authorized Administrator signatory.
  Note: Directional Letter may not be required for non-securitization accounts. Check AMA.

Step 2: Generate Invoice (Portfolio Management)
  Use: Invoice Template - UAM Reimbursable Expense.xlsm
  Enter details, save as PDF. Upload to "Invoices" folder in Account folder.

Step 3: Request Bid Funds from Trustee (Portfolio Management)
  Draft email to Trustee requesting wire to Unlock.
  Attach: signed Directional Letter (if applicable), Invoice PDF, target wire date.
  CC: Chief Capital Officer, Chief Credit Officer, Head of Customer Success,
      Head of Treasury & Investment Operations, Director of Capital Markets,
      Head of Portfolio Management, Special Servicing Lead,
      Investment Operations and Treasury Lead, Treasury Team.

Step 4: Request Bid Wire Setup (Portfolio Management, after funds confirmed received)
  Investment Operations provides Fed confirmation number.
  PM team member sends wire setup request to Billing specifying:
  - Wire amount
  - Recipient (auction representative)
  - Purpose of funds
  - Targeted processing date
  Attach recipient's wire instructions. Same CC list as Step 3.
  Notify Investment Operations once wire setup request sent.

Step 5: Release of Wire (Investment Operations / Financial Operations)
  Investment Operations obtains approvals.
  Once approved and executed, provides Fed confirmation number to PM team.
  Confirms funds sent to auction representative.
